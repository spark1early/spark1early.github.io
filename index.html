001 — About

Not a tech bro.
Not a pro gamer.
Just someone who
figured out AI.

I'm a consistent casual gamer, independent musician, and AI consultant based in Boston. I've been producing music for over 20 years — East Coast Hip-Hop, R&B, Afro Beats — and released my first artist project in 2023 under the name Spark1Early.

Somewhere between delivering packages and doing catering gigs, I figured out that AI isn't a search engine. It's a conversation. And once you understand that, everything changes. I started using it to build things I couldn't have built alone — apps, strategies, systems, content pipelines.

Now I help small businesses and independent artists do the same thing. Get out of the mud. Get on solid ground. Once you can walk, you can run.
